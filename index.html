Northeast USA is a beautiful place to visit. During any season there are a plethora of activities in every state and town. The weather temperature can fluctuate drastically with up to 30 degrees F in the summer and up to 50 degrees F in the winter within a 24 H period. Winters can be very cold but are also beautiful.
There are plenty of outdoor activities in the winter, such as ice scatting, ice fishing, winter hunting, camping, skiing and more. They all range in elevation and terrain to your desire and ski skills.
